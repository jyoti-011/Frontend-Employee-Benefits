DASHBOARD BENEFIT PROGRAMS WORK LIFE BALANCE DETAILS

Work Life Balance

Specifications

Flexible Work Options: Work from home, flexible hours

Parental Leaves: Maternity, paternity, and adoption leaves

Benefits of Work Life Balance

Improved Productivity: Employees are more productive when they have a good work-life balance

Employee Satisfaction: Increases job satisfaction and retention

Health and Well-being: Promotes mental and physical health

Reduced Stress: Helps in managing stress and burnout

How to Request Work Life Balance Benefits

Check Eligibility: Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for the benefits

Submit Request: Fill out the online request form

Approval Process: Request will be reviewed and approved by the HR department

Avail Benefits: Start availing the work-life balance benefits upon approval

Tips for Maintaining Work Life Balance

Set Boundaries: Define clear boundaries between work and personal time

Take Breaks: Take regular breaks to avoid burnout

Prioritize Tasks: Focus on important tasks and delegate when possible

Stay Organized: Keep your work and personal life organized
